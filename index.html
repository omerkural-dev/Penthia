Core Features

Why choose Penthia.

4K Visual Clarity

Ultra-high resolution helps diagrams, media, documents, and lesson content remain sharp and easy to follow across the room.

Touch + Stylus Input

Natural interaction through precise touchscreen control and stylus support gives teachers a smooth way to write, annotate, and guide instruction.

Flexible OS Options

The Vertex Elite includes Android 15 and Windows OPS as standard. The Vertex Pro runs Android 14 by default with optional Windows expansion. The Vertex Standard keeps things focused with Android 14 only.

Easy Installation

Each board supports wall mounting and stand placement, making deployment easier across classrooms, labs, training rooms, and meeting spaces.

Built to Last

Penthia displays are designed for the demands of daily classroom use, with durable hardware intended for consistent long-term performance.

Educator-First Design

From screen size to platform flexibility, every decision in the lineup is shaped around what teachers and institutions actually need in real use.
