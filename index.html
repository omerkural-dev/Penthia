Core Features

Why choose Penthia.

4K Visual Clarity

Ultra-high resolution ensures every detail - from diagrams to video - is crisp and clear for every student in the room.

Touch + Stylus Input

Natural interaction through precise touchscreen control and full stylus support - write, annotate, and present effortlessly.

Flexible OS Options

The Vertex Elite ships with Android 15 and Windows OPS included as standard. The Vertex Pro runs Android 14 by default with an optional Windows OPS upgrade. The Vertex Standard is a focused Android 14 platform - clean, capable, and ready from day one.

Easy Installation

Every board supports wall mounting and stand placement - designed for any room layout or deployment environment.

Built to Last

Penthia displays are engineered for daily classroom use - robust hardware built to perform consistently across full school years.

Educator-First Design

Every specification decision - from screen size to OS flexibility - is made with real classroom environments and teachers in mind.
