Current focus. Long-term ambition.

Penthia’s immediate mission is to establish a strong presence through premium smartboard products. Over time, the brand can evolve into a more advanced classroom platform with connected software, stronger control systems, and intelligent educational workflows.

Phase 01

Launch a strong product identity around iBoard Pro and begin presenting it professionally to schools and decision-makers.

Phase 02

Expand into a fuller software experience around lecture control, classroom support, content management, and more polished workflows.

Phase 03

Develop a broader intelligent classroom ecosystem under the Penthia brand with premium educational technology at its center.
